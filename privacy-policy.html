Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 16, 2026

UTC Solana Mobile is published by Joshua07q. This app displays UTC time information and can connect to a compatible Solana wallet for wallet-aware features.

Data We Process

We may process public wallet addresses and approved on-chain activity that are needed to support wallet connection and related features. We do not intentionally collect private keys or wallet seed phrases.

On-Chain Activity

Any blockchain activity that you approve through your wallet is public on the Solana network. Wallet addresses and transaction signatures may remain publicly visible.

Local App Data

The web app and native app may keep temporary wallet session data on your device to support reconnecting during active use.

Third Parties

The app depends on Solana RPC infrastructure, Solana Mobile Wallet Adapter compatible wallets, and mobile platform services required to deliver the app.
